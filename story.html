The Story of Sing Khao


Authenticity, tradition, and flavor are what we stand by to provide to our customers.

We are a Thai cuisine restaurant that focuses on northeastern Thai food, also known as Thai Isan. It is part of Thailand that boarders the famous Mekong River in Southeast Asia. Our food is inspired by the traditional recipes we grew up eating, brought to us from our parents that lived near the region.

We hope to bring an authentic atmosphere to each and every one of you as if you were family. Enjoy the flavors of northeastern Thailand and the essence of its people. ♥

The restaurant was named after my father. Sing translates to lion, and Khao simply translates to food or rice. Naming the restraurant after a parent is a reminder that there is no other love like theirs, but we can try to make food that reminds us of them and how they showed their love through food.
